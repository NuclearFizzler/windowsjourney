If you use a desktop computer or a laptop, you are most likely using Windows right now. It's the most popular computer operating system for home computers and in the workplace. At the time of writing, Windows computers currently make up a majority of all desktops and laptops worldwide.

![Graph of comparative OS market shares. Windows sits at 70% of all computers.]({{site.baseurl}}/img/beginnings/os_stats.png)
*Source: <https://gs.statcounter.com/os-market-share/desktop/worldwide>*  

<br />

Even though most people have experience using Windows for everyday tasks, learning how to *manage and secure* a Windows computer is something that most people don't think too hard about. Even if you don't want to make a career out of cybersecurity, learning about the different things Windows does will equip you with the knowledge to secure yourself.
